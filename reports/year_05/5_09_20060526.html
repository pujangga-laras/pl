Year 5, #9

Friday, 28 May 2006
Home of Ibu Cendhani Laras, Baluwarti, Solo

Dear Friends,

Last Friday night we had the 9th klenengan in the 5th fiscal year of Pujangga Laras at the home of Ibu Cendhani Laras in Baluwarti. It started at 8 pm and went until 2:00 am. We didn't have a student group play this month, as the addition of this klenengan was at the last minute. We will have the 10th klenengan in one more week on Saturday night, 3 June and perhaps will have a student group then.

It was a somewhat smaller group, due to the last minute nature of setting the date perhaps. But the klenengan had a very nice, intimate feel and our beloved Pak Harjito was there along with Ibu Deni, as well as Bp. Midiyanto from Berkeley/Eromoko, which made it very special.

Pak Dalimin is much better, but still didn't feel up to going out at night. The one piece of bad news we have to report is that Ibu Sri Yanti, who in past years (particularly at Laweyan) had come off and on to the klenengan, passed away recently. She was only in her early 40's, but had been struggling with some sort of stomach or liver problem for years.

Of course writing this report now, the strangest thing about this month's klenengan was that 3-4 hours after we all went home, the earthquake hit, and Solo was rocked quite violently. Mas Wakidi and I had only been asleep about 3 hours when we were awakened by the extremely strong rocking and jostling. At the time of writing this we haven't heard of any musician friends with damage to their homes, much less to their families, and hope that everyone is OK.
