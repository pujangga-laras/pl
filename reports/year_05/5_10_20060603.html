Year 5, #10

Saturday, 3 June 2006
Home of Bp. Rahayu Supanggah, Benawa, Palur

This report was written many months later in this February of 2007, reconstructed from my handwritten records, when I realized I had never sent the June report! This June klenengan took place only a week or so before I left for the USA for Ki Purbo Asmoros June 2006 wayang tour, and now looking back I cant even believe I was in Solo organizing the klenengan at that late date, let alone writing a report afterwards! So many, many apologizes for the 8 months-late report. On the other hand, either most of you are too nice to mention the missing report, or it didnt matter in the short term anyway, as only two people brought it to my attention! Actually it was a very interesting klenengan and important to document on paper because of all the new gendhing. Gendhing by Bp-s Wakidjo, Suyadi, and Harjito were featured.

On June 3, a Saturday night, we had the 10th and final klenengan in the 5th fiscal year of Pujangga Laras at the home of Bp Rahayu Supanggah in Benawa, Palur. It started at 8:20 pm and went until 2:30 am.
