Year 9, #2

5 December 2009
Home of Bp. Pahang, Windhan, Makam Haji

On Saturday night, 5 December 2009, we had the second klenengan in the 9th fiscal year running. This klenengan was held in Windhan, Makam Haji, at the home of Bp. Pahang to commemorate the two-year anniversary of the death of his mother, Ibu Naryo. It turned out to be a very quiet klenengan with not many guests, for a number of reasons.

  • It was raining a major storm in Klaten, so Bp. Wito/Ibu Dewi and the entire group they were planning on bringing could not attend.
  • Bp. Panggah ended up having a guest from Singapore, unexpected, so an entire entourage from Benawa was unable to attend.
  • Some of our major sindhen were unable to attend for individual reasons—Ibu Yayuk and Ibu Yatmi both had separate engagements they could not decline, as did Bu Harjutri.

However, we did have the attendance of two musicians who very rarely are able to attend—Ki Jungkung Dharmoyo and Bp. Mulyanto. Ki Jungkung Dharmoyo is a dhalang (and rebab player) and the son of the famous, deceased Ki Mujoko Rahardjo of Gombang. Bp. Mulyanto is his older cousin and a kendhang player, son of Bp. Karno, Ki Mujoko’s older brother and a gender player.

The klenengan started at 8:15 and ran through to 2:00 am. First few gendhing planned by Bp. Darsono, ISI. We spent Rp. 7,565,000 or $763 (Rp. 9,350= $1).

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
