Year 9, #10

19 July 2010
SMK Negeri 8, Kepatihan, Surakarta

On Monday, 19 July 2010 we had the tenth klenengan in the 9th fiscal year running.

This was the second half of our “TENTH YEAR OPENING” celebration, which had started in a klenengan earlier that day as well. Although the tenth year has not quite yet started, we chose to have the celebration during the July month, as more foreigners who support the klenengan tend to be in town. Apologies to those who left before this day, or arrived afterwards—it was very difficult to schedule!

The afternoon and evening klenengan were both held in the pendhopo of “SMK Negeri 8” in Kepatihan, Solo, which used to be known as SMKI and before that Konservatori (“Konser”), and is basically Solo’s High School of the Performing Arts.

The gendhing were planned by Bp-s Wakidjo, Darsono “Jepang”, and Wakidi. (But unfortunately Bp. Darsono “Jepang” had a rehearsal and was unable to come at the last minute—he came to watch the morning klenengan.)

The evening started with Ki Sri Joko Rahardjo’s youth group known as Parikesit performing a few pieces. I was so busy running around that I didn’t jot down what they played, and they weren’t recorded either.

Of course the MAJOR highlight of this klenengan was the presence of Ibu Tukinem, who had not attended a musical event in YEARS. We were also very happy to have Pak Sumarsam play gender for a number of pieces!

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
