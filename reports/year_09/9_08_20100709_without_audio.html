Year 9, #8

9 July 2010
ISI Surakarta

On Friday night, 9 July 2010 we had the eighth klenengan in the 9th fiscal year running. This klenengan was held in the pendhopo of ISI (Institute Seni Indonesia) in Kentingan and was in celebration of ISI’s Founder’s Day. Bp. Raji was the organizer of the gendhing.

Three gender players tonight and all new! (Bp-s Sumarsam, Slamet Riyadi, and AL Suwardi!)

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
