Year 9, #11

24 September 2010
Home of Dr. Rahayu Supanggah, Benawa, Palur

On Friday, 24 September 2010 we had the eleventh klenengan in the 9th fiscal year running. It was at the home of Dr. Rahayu Supanggah in Benawa, Palur.

The gendhing this month were put together by Bp. Wakidi. A special addition was the Pujangga Laras premiere of Ki Jungkung Darmoyo’s garapan for the classical Gd. Okrak-Okrak.

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta

Budget

We spent Rp. 8,800,000 or $988, (Rp.8,900= $1). The expense of this klenengan was about 30% above normal expense, as we handed out Lebaran bonuses to the musicians. (Lebaran is the holiday celebrated in Java at the end of the fasting month, and it is traditional to give bonuses to anyone who regularly recieves income from you.)
