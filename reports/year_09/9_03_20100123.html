Year 9, #3

23 January 2010
Home of Bp. Rahayu Supanggah, Benawa, Palur

On Saturday night, 23 January 2010 we had the third klenengan in the 9th fiscal year running.

The klenengan started at 8:15 and ran through to 2:00 am. First the first time, the first few gendhing planned by a few members of the committee planning Pujangga Laras. Unfortunately, the musicians felt afterwards that they hadn’t played too well, and asked to repeat a few of the gendhing next month….

But once the klenengan got going it was quite fun anyway, lots of activity and joking around. One of those very social times. One of the few recordings with mas Wakidi on gender and Bp. Darsono “Jepang” on kendhang (Malarsih-Gonjing Miring). Some very, very funny musical bloopers on this recording, and lots of laughter.

(Now looking back, this was the last time Pak Dalimin was recorded before his passing away.)

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
