Year 13, #3

24 March 2014
Home of Ki Purbo Asmoro, Gebang, Kadipiro, Solo

(Note: This klenèngan took place during a period of time when I was very, very busy, and I did not write the report until June 2018, using notes I had. I did not access the recording while writing the report, but just wrote up what I had written. If anyone listens to the recording and has corrections or additions, please send them via email. I caught up on reports also during a very busy time, and only went so far as to write up what I had written.)

On Tuesday 24 March 2014 we had the third klenèngan in the 13th year of Pujangga Laras. It was held at the home of Ki Purbo Asmoro, Gebang, Kadipiro, Solo and started at 8:00 finishing at 1:30.

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
