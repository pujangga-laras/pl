Year 1, #1

Saturday, July 21, 2001
Kerajinan Gamelan Bp. Mulyadi, Lojiwetan, Solo

Here follows a full report on the klenengan, for those who are hungry for all the details. For those who aren't, feel free to skip all this -- it's pretty detailed and for real fanatics only! Thanks again for your support and be assured that the Solonese musicians appreciate it very much.

Acara Gendhing

  1. Gd. Bn. MAJEMUK , pl 5

    Bn: Dalimin
    Kd: Yadi

  2. Gd. REBENG, pl 6 pt. Lasem

    Rb: Panggah
    Gd: Kamso
    Kd: Yadi

  3. Gd. MOROSONJO kalj Ldr. SOBRANG, sl 6 pt. Wantah

    Rb: Panggah
    Gd.: Kamso
    Kd: Yadi, penuntung Saptono

  4. Gd. ELA-ELA PENGANTEN, pl 6 pt. Jugag

    Rb: Wahyo
    Gd: Karno/RRI
    Kd Gd: Saptono
    Ciblon: Wakidjo

  5. Jn. Uler Kambang, sl 9
    Gd. GAMBIRSAWIT kalj. Ldr. KEMBANG TANJUNG, irama wilet, Pt. Jingking dados Ayak-Ayakan Gadhung Mlathi, Palaran Pangkur, Durma, Asmaradana, Pangkur Suryagreged

    Rb: Wahyo
    Gd: Panggah
    Kd Gd: Saptono
    Ciblon: Wakidjo
    Palaran: Mulyani, Sastratoegiyo, Mujiono, Darsono/STSI

  6. Jn. Mari Kangen
    Jn. Mijil
    Jn. Klambi Lurik
    Lgm. Ngimpi

    Gd: Wakidjo
    Kd; Wakidi

  7. Bawa Maduretna dhawah Gd. LOBONG mg KINANTHI diseling PUSPANJANA kalj. Ldr. KEMBANG PEPE dados AYAK-AYAKAN MIJIL LARASATI, sl. manyura

    Bawa: Sastratoegiyo
    Rb: Saptono
    Gd: Wakidjo
    Kd Gd. Yadi
    Ciblon: Mujiono
