Year 1, #6

Saturday, 8 December 2001
Kerajinan Gamelan Bp. Mulyadi, Lojiwetan, Solo

Last night we had the sixth of the monthly American-sponsored klenengan. It took place at Bapak Mulyadi's in Lojiwetan, as usual, and started a bit late at 8:30 pm, due to people running late after their post-fasting meal. The musicians finished at about 2 am. It was PACKED again--like last month--as this is still the fasting month and there are basically no other events going on. Thankfully, no one seemed to have forgotten that we moved it up a week!

Pak Sastratoegiyo even made it this month. Bp. Sagimin (bonang player from RRI) and Bp. Wardjina (SriWedari) joined us. We also had a new sindhen come--Sri Yanti. She's a sindhen originally from Ngawi, East Java, who came to Solo in her youth to study sindhenan with Ibu Sukini/Mangkunegaran. She's now a sindhen with the "superstar"/popstar/cult figure dhalang "Slenk", one of Anom Soeroto's younger brothers. She's rarely free and may have only come this once because it's the fasting month, but she clearly expressed a desire to "get back to the classic stuff" and appreciated the opportunity.
