Year 3, #3

Saturday, 20 September 2003
Laweyan, Solo

Dear Friends,

Last night we had the 3rd klenengan of the 3rd fiscal year of our event. As usual, it was quite full! It’s interesting that there’s nothing specific to report this time—no new players, and no particular “theme”, jokes, or personalities that stuck out. It was just a really solid klenengan, and quite a few commented one of the best in feeling. (Bu Yatmi’s rendition of Dhendhang Semarang was my personal favorite moment, but there were many wonderful moments.)

The STSI crowd—Bp.-s Kamso, Raji, and Pardi (and perhaps Bp. Panggah?) were not able to come because they were in France. Bp. Darsono “Jepang” had another event to go to, and Bp. Saptono, and a few other regulars from the Kraton, were attending a rehearsal of the Bedhaya Ketawang at the Kraton. Bp. Darsono “cilik” is in Malaysia for a month. Bp. Bambang Siswanto is spending most of his time in Klathen these days, where he has his own group (NOT Campursari!) that receives a lot of work, especially on Saturday nights. He told us when we saw him recently that he’d come whenever he could, and really enjoyed coming, but is often busy (yeah!).

A number of the musicians just happen to have one common conflict next month, so the next klenengan will be on Friday night, 17 October instead of the Saturday night, and this was announced to everyone.
