Year 6, #1

Saturday, 28 July 2006
Home of Bp. Rahayu Supanggah, Benawa, Palur

Dear Friends,

Thanks to those who have already done soâ€”please send in your subscriptions for the 06-07 year, which has already started!

About 10 days ago, on Saturday night, 28 July, we had the 1st klenengan in the SIXTH fiscal year of Pujangga Laras at the home of Bp Rahayu Supanggah in Benawa, Palur. It started at 8:20 pm and went until 2:30 am. There were a RECORD number of foreigners and listeners in general I would say, as it is vacation month. The best news to report is that Bu Tukinem was able to attend and even sang a bit. She is having a hard time financially and we gave her an extra envelope at the end of the evening (Rp. 500,000).

There was a small ceremony conducted by Bp. Waridi of STSI to commemorate the beginning of Pujangga Laras' 6th fiscal year, and also to serve as a small goodbye party for Marc Benamou who has been in Solo for 6 months and was going back to the USA in the next few days.

The August klenengan has yet to be scheduled, but will most likely be the last week of August.
