Year 6, #5a

Friday, 2 February 2007
Home of Ibu Cendhani in Baluwarti, Solo

Dear Friends,

Last night—Feb 2—we were scheduled to hold the 6th klenengan in this 6th fiscal year at the home of Ibu Cendhani in Baluwarti. It had to be cancelled however, due to terrible flash flooding in Jakarta and our inability to get to Solo.

On Thursday night the rain started and on Friday everything in Jakarta—including my school—was closed down due to meter-high water on all the roads. We were in a taxi for 4 hours and on standby for numerous flights after arriving late, but couldnt get to Solo at all on Friday. at 5 pm we called our helpers in Solo and told them to serve out the food to people who came (some people we were able to notify by cell phone), and tell them we couldnt make it and the klenengan was cancelled. A number of people decided to hang out and play anyway until about 11:30.
