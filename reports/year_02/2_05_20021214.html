Year 2, #5

Saturday, 14 December 2002
Laweyan, Solo

Dear Friends,

Last night we had the 5th klenengan of the 2nd fiscal year of our klenengan series. It started at 8:00 pm and went until 2:15 am. It was absolutely PACKED and a lot of fun for all. Of course last month we had a vacation for the fasting month (as did most other events) and everyone seemed happy to be back playing together.

We had a few new participants: Bp. Slamet, a young musician from SriWedari who directs a large number of hobby groups in town Bp. Hartono, a young gerong singer from the radio station Bp. Wardi, a 55-year-old musician from the Kraton, often plays kendhang there A young sindhen student from Bp. Ripto/RRIís groups

Budget

It is traditional after the fasting month, for people to receive ìLebaranî bonuses from anyone they normally have a working relationship with. The founding committee for this klenengan decided to give Lebaran bonuses in this situation as well (especially since we took last month off and had that money available), so we spent about twice as muchóRp. 7,320,000ówhich at the banks this week was $831 ($1 = Rp. 8800). If we count this as $415 for November and $415 for December, it falls comfortably within out target (max of $500 per klenengan).
