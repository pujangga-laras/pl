Year 8, #9

23 July 2009
Home of Bp. Supanggah, Benawa, Palur

On Thursday night, 23 July 2009, we had the 9th klenengan in the 8th fiscal year running. This klenengan was quite full, due to the many foreigners who happened to be in town.

It was the second klenengan that we have had on Pak Panggah’s newly acquired gamelan from Kudus. The group is still struggling with finding gender players who enjoy playing the gender—all of which have high 5’s. Pak

Pak Wakidjo organized the first four gendhing, through Gd Renyep gedhe.

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
