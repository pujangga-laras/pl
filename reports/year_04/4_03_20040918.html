Year 4, #3

Saturday 18 September 2004

Laweyan, Solo

Dear Friends,

Last night we had the 3rd klenengan in the 4th fiscal year of the Laweyan klenengan. It started at 8:15 pm and ended at 2:00 am. Some very large and serious gendhing had been chosen for the initial set gendhing and the mood was very staid in the beginning. The first piece alone (Gonjanganom)—no suite involved and followed only by pathet jugag—lasted 62 minutes! Once Joko Ngiwat ended, and Stuart Frankel and Gary Harmon brought out some sweet wine, the mood changed on a dime and the musicians broke into the suite of jineman. Then the Mataraman suite, the dance-related suite, and the Asmaradana/garapan Pangkur gala-gala made for a wild last 2 hours.

Most musicians were in attendance.
