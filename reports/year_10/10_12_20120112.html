Year 10, #12

11 January 2012
Home of Ki Purbo Asmoro, Gebang, Kadipiro, Surakarta

On 11 January 2012 we had the final klenengan in the TENTH year of Pujangga Laras. It was held at the home of Ki Purbo Asmoro in Gebang, Kadipiro, Surakarta. The gendhing were set by a member of the founding committee of Pujangga Laras, anonymously.

(This report was written QUITE late—11 March 2013—based on notes in Kitsie’s book…)

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta

Acara Gendhing

Gd Bn Slébrak kt 4 arang mg 8, pl lima

bn: Mardi
kd: Wakidjo

Ktw Gd Daradasih kt 4 kr mg kalj Ldr Kagok, pathetan jugag, pl lima

rb: Raji
gd: Kamso
kd: Yadi
bn: Legiyo
gb: Wakiyo

Ktw Gd Krawitan kt 4 kr mg Ldr (wilet), pathetan wantah, sl nem

rb: Raji
gd: Kamso
kd: Yadi
bn: Mardi
gb: Wakiyo

Gd Sambul Gendhing kt 4 kr mg 8 kalj Ldr Sambul, pl nem

rb: Darsono “cilik”
gd: pl 6 Sumarsam, pl 7 Kamso
kd: Yadi
ciblon: Wakidjo
bn: Mardi
gb: Wakiyo

Ktw Gd Ela-ela Kalibeber kt 2 kr mg 4 dados Ayak-ayak rangkep mawi Palaran Pangkur Paripurna, Dhandhanggula Temanten Anyar (Iyasa), Asmaradana Matarman (Yayuk), Durma (Hartono), Mijil (Parni) dados Srepeg Mataraman, pathetan jugag, sl sanga

rb: Mujiono ganti Raji
gd: Kamso
kd: Wakidjo
bn: Mardi
gb: Tulus
