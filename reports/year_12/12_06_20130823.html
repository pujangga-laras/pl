Year 12, #6

23 August 2013
Home of Prof Dr Rahayu Supanggah, Benawa, Ngringo, Karanganyar

On Friday, 23 August 2013, we had the SIXTH klenengan in the 12th year of Pujangga Laras. It was held at the home of Prof Dr Rahayu Supanggah, in Benawa, Ngringo, Karanganyar. It started at 8 pm, and ended at 2:00 am. It was a special klenengan, because all the pieces were arranged by Pujangga Laras advisor, as well as our teacher, and friend Bp IM Harjito, who was in attendance from Wesleyan.

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
