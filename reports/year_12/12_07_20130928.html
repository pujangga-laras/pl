Year 12, #7

28 Sept 2013
Home of Ki Purbo Asmoro, Sanggar Seni Mayangkara, Gebang, Kadipiro, Surakarta

On Saturday, 28 Sept 2013, we had the SEVENTH klenengan in the 12th year of Pujangga Laras. It was held at the home of Ki Purbo Asmoro, in Gebang. It started at 8 pm, and ended at 2:00 am. The pieces were set by Bapak Wakidi.

This klenengan was small—many musicians had other engagements on a Saturday night before the month of Sura. But it was quite special somehow too, intimate. Two highlights:

  • Bapak Karna from the Kraton (normally a bedhayan kendhang player) played gender, and beautifully, for much of the night.
  • Bapak Giarto, a gerong singer with the late Ki Nartasabda and the late Ki Mujoko, who currently sings with Ki Manteb Soedharsono, has decided to join our gerong section when he’s free. He is also an excellent suling player.

NEW POLICY IN THE NAME OF HEALTH:

BTW: We have been unable to start our new policy on health for various complicated reasons. This month we were going to make the announcement, but it was an odd group—new members and yet a lot of the old ones not able to come. Just better to make the “no free cigarettes” announcement next month.

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
