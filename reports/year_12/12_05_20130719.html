Year 12, #5

19 July 2013
ISI Surakarta, Kentingen, Jebres

On Friday, 19 July 2013, we had the FIFTH klenengan in the 12th year of Pujangga Laras. It was held in the pendhopo of ISI Solo, as part of the Founder’s Day week of celebrations. It started at 8 pm, and ended at 2:00 am.

The pieces were been set by the Department Head of Karawitan at ISI Solo, and longterm member of Pujangga Laras, Bapak Suraji.

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
