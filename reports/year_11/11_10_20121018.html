Year 11, #10

18 October 2012
Home of Ki Purbo Asmoro, Gebang, Kadipiro, Surakarta

On 18 October 2012, just three days after our last klenengan (we were trying to get caught up!) we had the TENTH klenèngan in the 11th year running of Pujangga Laras. The klenèngan was held at the home of Ki Purbo Asmoro, in Gebang, Kadipiro, Surakarta

(This report was written in March 2013, based on my notes.)

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
