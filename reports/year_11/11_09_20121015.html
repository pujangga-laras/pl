Year 11, #9

15 October 2012
Home of Ki Bambang Suwarno, Sangkrah, Surakarta

On 15 October 2012, we had the NINTH klenèngan in the 11th year running of Pujangga Laras. The klenèngan was held for the very first time at the home of Ki Bambang Suwarno, in Sangkrah, Surakarta. Pak Bambang also requested Gd Kembang Mara, Ktw Gd Karawitan, and Gd Gendhu, while the rest of the gendhing were spontaneous.

(This report was written in March 2013, from my notes.)

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
