Year 11, #3

17 May 2012
Home of Ki Purbo Asmoro, Gebang, Kadipiro, Solo

On Thursday 17 May 2012, we had the THIRD klenèngan in the 11th year running of Pujangga Laras. The klenèngan was held at the home of Ki Purbo Asmoro in Gebang, Kadipiro, Solo. It began at 8:00 and ran through to 02:15 am. The gendhing were set by a member of the Pujangga Laras founding committee, anonomously again like last month.

(This report was written quite late—in March 2013—from notes in Kitsie’s book!)

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
