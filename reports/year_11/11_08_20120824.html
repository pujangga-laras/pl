Year 11, #8

24 August 2012
Home of Ibu Mulyani Cendhani Laras, Baluwarti, Solo

On 24 August 2012, we had the EIGHTH klenèngan in the 11th year running of Pujangga Laras. The klenèngan was held at the home of Ibu Mulyani Cendhani Laras in Baluwarti, Solo.

(This report was written long after the event—in March 2013—based on the recording ONLY, because my notes were missing from the book for this one.)

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
