Year 11, #11

16 November 2012
Home of Dr. Rahayu Supanggah, Benawa, Palur

On Friday, 16 November 2012, we had the ELEVENTH klenèngan in the 11th year running of Pujangga Laras. The klenèngan was held at the home of Dr. Rahayu Supanggah in Benawa.

The gendhing were set by Pak Panggah.

(This report was written in March 2013, based on my notes.)

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
