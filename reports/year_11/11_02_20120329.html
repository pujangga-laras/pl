Year 11, #2

29 March 2012
Home of Dr. Rahayu Supanggah, Benawa, Palur

On Thursday 29 March 2012, we had the SECOND klenèngan in the 11th year running of Pujangga Laras. The klenèngan was held at the home of Dr. Rahayu Supanggah in Benawa, Palur. It began at 8:30 and ran through to 02:15 am, and the gendhing were set by a member of the Pujangga Laras founding committee.

Some surprise players this klenengan, like in Gd Gendrèh, sl manyura, Bp Sular on ciblon and Bp Kuat on rebab.

(Report written QUITE late—about a full year later—in March 2013 from Kitsie’s notes)

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
