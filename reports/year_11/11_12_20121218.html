Year 11, #12

18 December 2012
Sanggar Seni Mayangkara, Gebang, Kadipiro, Surakarta

On Tuesday, 18 December 2012, we had the TWELFTH and last klenèngan in the 11th year running of Pujangga Laras. The klenèngan was held at the home of Ki Purbo Asmoro at Sanggar Seni Mayangkara, in Gebang, Kadipiro, Surakarta.

The gendhing were completely freeform and unplanned.

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
