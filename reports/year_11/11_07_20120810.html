Year 11, #7

10 August 2012
Home of Ki Purbo Asmoro, Gebang, Kadipiro, Solo

On 10 August 2012, we had the SEVENTH klenèngan in the 11th year running of Pujangga Laras. The klenèngan was held at the home of Ki Purbo Asmoro in Gebang, Kadipiro, Solo. We had a very special guest at this klenèngan—Ibu Ngatirah from Semarang was able to come, along with Pak Tanto, a musician friend also from Wayang Orang Ngèsthi Pandhawa in Semarang. Thanks to Jen Shyu, who helped finance this and made it possible!

(This report was written 6 months after the klenengan—in March 2013—and my notes were a bit sketchy. If anyone accessing the recording or present at the klenengan has anything to add or correct please notify us—thanks.)

—Kitsie Emerson, Solo/Jakarta
